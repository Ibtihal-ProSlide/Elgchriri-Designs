Website Design
05 Web Design + Development

BrownieVerse — Premium E-commerce Website

The Challenge

A Start-up Moroccan Brand needed a high-converting website that reflected their premium positioning while providing an intuitive shopping experience across all devices.

The Solution

End-to-end brand system + conversion-focused ordering experience, designed to position BrownieVerse as a premium artisan brand in Morocco's competitive dessert market. Delivered a culturally-rooted visual identity (Moroccan-global fusion aesthetics), a streamlined "Commandi Online" ordering flow with clear delivery windows, and WhatsApp integration for <60s order confirmations. Built the "Pirate Crew" loyalty program to drive retention through exclusivity, lifetime price locks, and behind-the-scenes access — turning one-time buyers into brand advocates.

The Impact

Established BrownieVerse as Salé's premium artisan brownie brand, enabling premium pricing in a competitive market. The strategic ordering system and "Pirate Crew" loyalty program drove 100+ early customers and 50+ repeat buyers within the first launch cycle. WhatsApp integration achieved <60s confirmation times, building trust and reducing order abandonment.
